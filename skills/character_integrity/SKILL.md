# character_integrity

Purpose: use when characters appear to move the plot for the author instead of acting from what they know, fear, and can do.

Tools:
- `review_character_integrity`
- `review_plot_logic`
- `rewrite_by_plan`

Recommended order:
1. Check whether each major act matches current information and present pressure.
2. Verify the causal chain with `review_plot_logic`.
3. Rewrite behavior so the move grows from motive, fear, status, and trigger.

Hard rules:
- Do not let a character speak or act beyond what they currently know.
- If a risky move happens, the trigger must be on the page.
- Prefer indirection, hesitation, testing, and compromise over sudden perfect moves.
