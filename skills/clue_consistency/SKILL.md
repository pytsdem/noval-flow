# clue_consistency

Purpose: prevent clue airdrops and keep every clue tied to a valid source and dramatic function.

Tools:
- `review_clue_origin`
- `rewrite_by_plan`
- `final_judge`

Recommended order:
1. Check clue origin.
2. Rewrite to anchor every clue in source, holder, timing, and double duty.

Hard rules:
- No unexplained records, witnesses, relics, or testimony.
