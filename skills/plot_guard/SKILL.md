# plot_guard

Purpose: keep power limits, procedural logic, and causal flow believable.

Tools:
- `review_plot_logic`
- `review_continuity`
- `rewrite_by_plan`
- `final_judge`

Recommended order:
1. Check plot logic.
2. Cross-check continuity if the logic touches prior facts.
3. Rewrite without expanding authority or coincidence.
