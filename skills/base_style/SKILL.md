# base_style

Purpose: keep the chapter inside the established restrained historical-romance style card.

Tools:
- `review_prose_quality`
- `rewrite_by_plan`
- `final_polish`

Recommended order:
1. Check prose and tension.
2. Rewrite only inside the fixed information package.
3. Do a final polish after the chapter passes judge.

Hard rules:
- Do not add facts.
- Do not turn subtext into explanation.
- Keep POV limited and emotionally pressurized.
