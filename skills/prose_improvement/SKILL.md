# prose_improvement

Purpose: fix flat prose, weak tension, over-exposition, and low-subtext passages.

Tools:
- `review_prose_quality`
- `rewrite_by_plan`
- `final_polish`

Recommended order:
1. Inspect prose scores.
2. Rewrite toward stronger pressure, scene action, and double-duty detail.
3. Polish only after the scores are back above threshold.

Hard rules:
- Increase tension through action, pressure, and consequence.
- Do not fix prose by adding new plot facts.
