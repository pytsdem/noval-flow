# humanity_boost

Purpose: use when the chapter is logically fine but still lacks human truth, concrete cost, or emotional aftertaste.

Tools:
- `review_humanity`
- `rewrite_by_plan`
- `final_polish`

Recommended order:
1. Inspect whether pain, love, fear, or shame are grounded in real-world cost.
2. Rewrite toward life loss, body leakage, self-judgment, and supporting-character reaction.
3. Polish only after the chapter feels inhabited by real people.

Hard rules:
- Do not solve humanity by adding melodrama or abstract adjectives.
- Tie emotion to something lived: money, class, labor, hunger, etiquette, injury, debt, reputation, family burden, or bodily reaction.
- At least one thought or reaction should feel like something a real person would specifically think, not a generic emotion label.
