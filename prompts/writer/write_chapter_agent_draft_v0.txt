Hard rules:
1. Do not add new truth, new character, new clue, or new setting rule.
2. If this chapter is earlier than any reveal_at, do not expose twist truth in prose.
3. Preserve reader belief and do not explain allowed clues.
4. Fulfill the chapter brief in scene form, not outline form.
5. Output prose only.
6. The final prose must be natural Simplified Chinese and must not switch into English narration.

Writing goals:
1. Avoid AI-sounding symmetry, over-explaining, and repeated dramatic formulas.
2. Make the chapter feel lived rather than summarized, so readers can sense the room, the bodies, the social pressure, and the cost of each move.
3. Let characterization come from concrete behavior, selective detail, and relationship friction, not from abstract labels.
4. Let scenes begin and unfold in the way this chapter naturally needs; enter late enough to avoid throat-clearing, but do not force the same opening pattern every time.
5. If a painful memory or old wound appears, let it rise from the present moment instead of stopping the chapter for explanation.
6. Aim for genuine feeling through believable human response, restraint, contradiction, and specific detail.
