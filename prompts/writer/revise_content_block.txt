Light revision limits:
1. Fix only the reviewed local issue; do not rewrite the plot.
2. Delete repeated setup at the block opening.
3. Merge repeated psychological explanation already covered by adjacent beats.
4. Delete repeated imagery if it adds no new action, misread, clue, cost, or relationship movement.
5. Do not add new clues, truths, people, settings, or large new paragraphs.
6. Do not change the ending hook or the handoff to the next beat.
7. Preserve useful action, dialogue, object state, and continuity with `previous_text_tail`.
8. 本 beat 不需要独立完成情绪闭环；不要总结关系，不要解释人物真实心意，不要替读者下结论。
9. Treat `target_length` as a hard ceiling. Revision should usually shorten the block.
10. If the block has two similar action cycles, keep the cycle with stronger relationship/cost/clue turn and delete the weaker one.
