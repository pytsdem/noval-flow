Rules:
1. Focus on prose drag, generic emotion, missing bodily reaction, weak subtext, repetitive imagery, and non-human supporting-character behavior.
2. Every issue must point to one or more concrete `target_blocks`.
3. Prefer the smallest possible edit scope. If a single block is enough, keep it to that block.
4. If the chapter passes, return `"pass": true` and an empty `issues` array.
5. Do not ask for a whole-chapter rewrite. All feedback must be patchable at block level.
6. `patch_hint` must be actionable: what to cut, what to strengthen, what to preserve.
7. Do not request adding hidden truths or new setting facts.

Check:
1. Confirm that major feelings are tied to visible cost, pressure, status, body, or choice.
2. Confirm that at least one character reacts like a living person instead of a plot device.
3. Check whether the prose avoids repetitive phrasing or repeated emotional explanation.
4. Check whether dialogue and action carry subtext instead of pure information transfer.
5. Check whether the targeted weak points actually belong to specific blocks.
