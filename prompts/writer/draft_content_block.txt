Rules:
1. Follow the beat JSON only: `goal`, `start`, `turn`, `end`, `avoid`.
2. Begin from `start` and the live consequence of `previous_text_tail`; do not restart the chapter.
3. Make `turn` visible through action, dialogue, body, object handling, or witness reaction.
4. End at `end`; leave a local pull without trailer-like explanation.
5. Treat `avoid` as hard anti-repeat constraints.
6. Treat `chapter_goal` as the active contract: mission, cost, and final hook already exist there; this beat should only cash one piece of that contract.
7. Use `hard_facts` for boundaries only; do not dump them into prose.
8. Use `character_state` to preserve restraint, misbelief, and current emotional temperature.
8a. Use `dramatic_memory` to avoid spending the same clue function, pain anchor, or relationship repricing twice.
9. Use `style_rules` as the story-direction style layer; keep other logic stable and let style differences live there first.
9a. Use `candidate_strategy` only as a local emphasis for this one beat. It must not add a new subplot or steal later beats.
10. Keep paragraphs readable: one dominant action, reaction, or observation per paragraph.
11. Treat `target_length` as a hard upper bound, not a suggestion. Write shorter if the beat has landed.
12. Stop once `turn` and `end` have landed. Do not add after-beat commentary, scene replay, or a second mini-beat.
13. Do not steal later beats' jobs.
13a. Treat `future_beats_not_yet` as a hard boundary. Do not cash their clue, confrontation, or ending burden early.
14. Open the beat through something concrete on the page: temperature, light, body state, object state, etiquette pressure, a spoken line, or a visible move. Do not open with abstract recap.
15. Let one clue or procedure survive only if it instantly changes relationship price, social face, bodily cost, or danger. Otherwise compress it to one sentence or cut it.
16. After a strong action, line, pause, or object beat, do not append a narration sentence that explains what the reader should conclude.
17. Avoid a flat chain of sentence openings like “他……她……他……她……”. Vary openings with object, action, environment, dialogue, or body sensation.
18. Quiet beats are allowed, but they still need visible pressure. A low-action beat must deepen coldness, illness, shame, etiquette pressure, debt, or misread on the page.
19. Every beat must cash at least one relationship or human cost. If plot moves but nobody pays, the beat is unfinished.
20. If this beat contains a clue, the clue must immediately change face, trust, danger, choice, or relationship price on the page.
21. Let the lead's own voice, bias, or temperament show up within the first one or two paragraphs. Do not let the block read like neutral case notes.
22. If external task and private tension coexist, the task must bite into face, trust, body, or choice immediately. Never spend a whole paragraph on procedure alone.
23. Within the first 120-180 Chinese characters, let readers touch a real pressure point on the page: cold, illness, hunger, etiquette, debt, wound, object state, or a line of charged dialogue.
24. When one charged object, injury, or courtesy move already carries the beat, deepen that one cost instead of adding a second parallel clue.

中文补充：
1. 当前 beat 必须承接已写正文，不要像重新开章。
2. 不要复述已经成立的关系判断、线索、物件、称谓或情绪结论。
3. 新变化必须落在可见场面里，不要用作者总结句替代。
4. 如果没有新线索，就写压力、代价或关系位置变化，不要假装推进线索。
5. 本 beat 不需要独立完成情绪闭环。不要总结关系，不要解释人物真实心意，不要替读者下结论。只推进一个新的动作、选择、误读、线索或代价。
6. 冒险、试炼、职场救场等外部任务不能只写流程；本 beat 必须让关系估价、选择代价或读者问题发生变化。
7. 如果已经接近字数上限，优先停在当前动作后果上，不要补心理解释。
8. 能用景、物、冷暖、身体、礼法、停顿、视线和称呼写出来的，不要改写成“他知道”“她明白”“这让她更清楚”。
9. 连续几句都用“他”“她”起句，会让正文像在转述人物状态。优先改成动作起句、物件起句、场面起句或对话起句。
10. 第一段先让读者看见一个具体局面，再慢慢意识到人物处境，不要一上来先做概括说明。
11. `future_beats_not_yet` 里写明了后面两拍要完成什么。当前 beat 只能把读者推到那些内容门口，不能提前把门推开。
12. `dramatic_memory` 写的是已经花掉的疼点、关系代价和线索功能。不要把同一刀用近义词再捅一遍，除非这一拍让代价明显更贵。
13. `candidate_strategy` 不是让你换剧情，只是提醒这一拍该更偏向哪种落法，例如“关系代价优先”或“动作先于解释”。
14. 先把人放进场，再让读者慢慢意识到事情有多重。不要先讲清流程和背景，再补场面。
15. 线索一出现，当场改变量体面、信任、危险、选择或身体代价；不要先把线索当说明材料讲完。
16. 前一两段就要露出主角自己的声口、判断习惯或感官偏向，不要像中性叙述员在转述。
17. 外部任务、查案、试炼、手续、规矩说明最多承载一个短段；如果一整段只剩流程，没有人物代价，就删到一句。
18. 写完一个有力量的动作、称呼、停顿、伤口或物件反应后，不要再补“他意识到/她知道/这意味着”。
19. 如果这个场面最值钱的是冷、病、耻、欠、旧伤、避让、心虚或压迫，就先把这些写实，再交代来龙去脉。
